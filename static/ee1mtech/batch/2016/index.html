Autumn 2016 Seminar Topics

MTech students in the two year program (TAs, sponsored) need to do their seminar in the Autumn semester. MTech RAs will be doing in their seminars in the Spring 2017 semester. The following procedure can be adopted to decide on the seminar topic.

  • Meet faculty and discuss topics of interest. Email them for an appointment if you cannot find them in their office.
  • Finalize topic and get consent of the faculty member offering it. (Deadline: July 22, 2016)
  • Send an email to sarva@ee.iitb.ac.in with CC to concerned faculty. Include title of the seminar topic in your email.
